Troll Blooded Murder

A D&D Adventure

Troll Blooded Murder is a short murder mystery campaign for Dungeons and Dragons Fifth Edition. The campaign takes players to the town of Baryn's Field where a man has been found dead on his farm.  It's up to them to discover how this happened and bring those responsible to justice.  With twists and turns to keep players on their feet, this campaign is sure to provide players a fun experience as they unravel the truth.
  1. RECRUITING A PARTY - This campaign was designed for 4 players at 4th level, but may be adapted at the DM's discretion to fit the needs of most low level partys
  2. RESOURCES - Due to writing restrains, this adventure makes references to both the Fifth Edition Player's Handbook, and Monster Manual for statistics of items and monsters
